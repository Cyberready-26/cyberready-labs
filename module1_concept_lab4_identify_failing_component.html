"The NorthBridge operations desk is receiving customer complaints. Something is wrong — but the team needs to figure out what before they can fix it. Your job is to read each set of symptoms reported by customers and engineers, and identify which component in the architecture has failed."
Your Mission

You will work through 5 failure scenarios. Each scenario shows you a symptom log — what customers are reporting and what the monitoring system is detecting. Read the symptoms carefully, then click the component in the architecture diagram that you believe has failed.

After each scenario you will see an explanation of why that component was responsible. When all 5 scenarios are complete, the quiz unlocks.
