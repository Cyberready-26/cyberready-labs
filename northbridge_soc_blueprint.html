03
Compliance & Audit Layer — Regulatory Controls
Tamper-evident storage · Retention tiers · RBAC · Encryption · Regulatory mapping
PCI-DSS 10 RBI Audit ISO A.18 SOC2 CC7
▼
🔐 Tamper-Evident Log Storage
WORM storage with cryptographic hash chains. Write-once, read-many enforced. Every log entry is SHA-256 signed and chained to the previous.
PCI-DSS Req 10.5 RBI § 7.4
👥 RBAC Access Control
Role-based dashboard access enforced. SOC Analysts ≠ Dev Engineers ≠ Auditors. Least-privilege principle on all monitoring tools. MFA required for all access.
ISO A.9.2 SOC2 CC6.3
🔑 Encryption Policy
All telemetry encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Key management via HSM. Key rotation every 90 days. No plaintext secrets in config files.
PCI-DSS Req 4 ISO A.10.1
📅 Data Retention Policy
Three-tier retention with automated lifecycle management.
Hot 90d
Warm 1yr
Cold Archive 7yr
RBI § 9.2 SOC2 A1.2
